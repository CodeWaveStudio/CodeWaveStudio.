Instruction To Be Follow In Store Departmnet Login


  1. Open the web browser on your computer and navigate to the official Store Department login page.


  2. Enter your assigned username in the designated field. Please ensure that the username is entered accurately and without any spaces.


  3. Input your corresponding password in the provided password field. Make sure to type the password correctly, paying attention to uppercase and lowercase letters.


  4. Click on the "Login" button or press the "Enter" key on your keyboard to proceed.


  5. Once logged in, familiarize yourself with the store department's navigation and layout to locate the required sections and functionalities.


  6. Follow any specific instructions or guidelines provided by your supervisor or manager regarding tasks to be performed within the store department system.


  7. When you have completed your tasks or are finished using the store department login, remember to log out by clicking on the "Logout" button or option to ensure the security of your account and data.
